The Undead Famous Circus Show is a variety, costume, circus spectacular!
Impossible Things Entertainment invite you to pay homage to your deceased idols as we immerse you in a world of heroes past. Dress as your favourite posthumous person as our circus-cabaret acts help you pay tribute to pop cultures late greats!

Doors Open: 06:30pm, Show start 09:00pm & End 10:30pm

Please note: tickets are for admittance to the show only and do not include any food or drink.

Collection Instructions:
Collect your tickets directly from the Box Office, no later than 30 minutes before the event begins. Show your booking confirmation email (either on your phone, or a print out) and explain that you have booked via Central Tickets. Accept the seats that you are given without complaint. This is a 18+ venue, ID may be asked.
